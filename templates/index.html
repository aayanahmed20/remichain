How it works

  1. Donate — facilities list surplus medication, PPE, or equipment before it expires or gathers dust.
  2. Request — facilities in need post what they're short on, with an urgency level.
  3. Match — the engine scores every donation against every open request by urgency, expiry, distance, and quantity fit, and proposes the best pairing.
